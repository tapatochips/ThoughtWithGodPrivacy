Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Thoughts With God ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (used for account creation and receipts)
  • Username (chosen by you, displayed on the Prayer Board)
  • Prayer requests and discussion posts you choose to share
  • App preferences (theme, font size, Bible translation)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Push notification token (only if you grant permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Send purchase receipts and daily verse reminders
  • Display your username on community features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), a secure cloud database. Payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS compliant. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with service providers necessary for app functionality (Stripe, Google Firebase, Apple APNs).

6. Your Rights and Account Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can:

  • Update your username and preferences in Profile Settings.
  • Delete your account directly within the App via Profile Settings → Delete Account.
Web-Based Data Deletion: If you are unable to access the app and wish to request that your data be deleted, please fill out our Data Deletion Request Form.
